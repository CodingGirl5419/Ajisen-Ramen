About Us

It was established in 1968 in Kyushu, Japan. Today, Ajisen Ramen

totals over 700 branches all over the world. In New York, Ajisen

Ramen has three branches located in Chinatown Manhattan,

Midtown Manhattan and Flushing Queens.
